THE WONDER OF THE PHILIPPINES: A PHOTO GALLERY

El Nido

When it comes to pristine white sand beaches and islands, El Nido in Palawan comes highly-recommended by local and international travelers as the top choice to go to when in the Philippines.

Bicol Region

With loads of white-sand beaches, crumbling caves and epic waterfalls, the region of Bicol is one of the best places in the Philippines to explore. That being said, the biggest attraction is the nearby Catanduanes Mountains.


Boracay

Boracay is a relatively small island but has quickly become one of the best places in the Philippines to visit.

Banaue Rice Terraces

The Banaue Rice Terraces are a National Cultural Treasure of the Philippines and are considered to be the 8th Natural Wonder of the World by many Filipinos.
